# Privacy Policy for OptiFit

**Last Updated:** April 2024

## Overview

OptiFit is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

## Key Points

### We Don't Collect Personal Data
- No account required to use the app
- No email addresses collected
- No usage tracking or analytics
- No third-party advertising

### Your Photos Stay On Your Device
- Equipment scan photos are processed locally
- Images are optionally uploaded to your own backend server
- No photos sent to our servers (we don't have any)

### Local-First Architecture
- All workout data stored locally on your device
- Optional self-hosted backend for advanced features
- You own and control all your data

## Data We Process

### Camera Access
- **What:** We access your camera to scan gym equipment
- **Why:** To detect and identify available workout equipment
- **Storage:** Images may be stored temporarily for processing, then deleted
- **Sharing:** Never shared with anyone

### Photo Library Access
- **What:** We can access your photos to select gym equipment images
- **Why:** Alternative to camera for equipment detection
- **Storage:** Selected images processed locally
- **Sharing:** Never shared

### Workout Data
- **What:** Your logged workouts, sets, reps, and progress
- **Where:** Stored in local device database
- **Backup:** Optional export to your own server
- **Sharing:** Only if you explicitly choose to share

## Self-Hosted Backend (Optional)

If you connect to a self-hosted OptiFit backend:
- Your backend URL and credentials are stored locally
- All API calls go directly to your server
- We have no access to this connection

## Third-Party Services

### Open Source Libraries
We use the following open source libraries:
- Capacitor (Apache 2.0)
- React (MIT)
- Various other libraries (see LICENSE file)

### No External APIs
- No Google Analytics
- No Firebase
- No Facebook SDK
- No advertising networks

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data:
- No data to download (it's already on your device)
- No account to delete
- No data to correct

If using a self-hosted backend, your rights depend on that server's configuration.

## Children's Privacy

OptiFit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app and on our GitHub repository.
